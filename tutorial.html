Nezuko Kamado is a fictional character from the popular manga and anime series called "Demon Slayer." She is the younger sister of the main protagonist, Tanjiro Kamado. Nezuko possesses unique abilities as a demon, as she was transformed into one after surviving an attack that claimed the lives of her family. Despite being a demon, Nezuko retains her compassion and protective nature, striving to help her brother in his quest to avenge their family and find a cure for her condition. Nezuko's character is known for her unwavering loyalty, fierce determination, and her ability to shrink her body to fit inside a small box, making her an endearing and integral part of the series.
